Karen MacPherson

Engineer, Teacher, and Creator


I am an instructor and engineer at Fullstack Academy of Code in NYC, a three month web-engineering bootcamp that focuses on teaching full stack JavaScript.

A bit about me


Hello friend! Welcome to my corner of the internet.

I'm a biologist turned web engineer with no small list of hobbies thanks to my short attention span. About a year ago, I made the decision to leave academia to pursue a career in web development. I took the plunge and enrolled at Fullstack Greatest decision of my life--I started a new career, made amazing friends, and met my wonderful boyfriend. After I graduated, I was honored to be asked to stay at Fullstack as an instructor. Didn't wanna leave anyway! Currently, I help to run the Foundations phase of Fullstack Academy, which involves creating curriculum content, working with students, and developing tools and systems to streamline the many admin processes.

I love my job, but I do have a life outside! I am a reptile-momma, hiker, reader, tinkerer, gamer, and tea addict. Lately I've also taken up water painting and writing short stories.

Take a look at some of my projects


GitFun

Gitfun is an interactive command line tool aimed at improving Git education. Written using Node, published as an npm module.

Dance Dance Evolution

Dance Dance Evolution is a version of StepMania that lives entirely in the browser. Built using the MEAN stack.

Reptiwatch

Simple app used to interface with a microcontroller that monitors the temperature and humidity in reptile enclosures.

Spatial Game Theory

Simulations of spatial game theory, exploring the consequences of varying allele frequency of cooperation and deceit in Scala.

Here's what i'm into


Coding and Technology—Coding is a superpower in today's world, and is an art as much as a tool. My core superpowers include Node, Express, SQL, Mongo, Angular, and React.

Education—Because coding is a superpower, it's important that people have the opportunity to get a coding education even if they don't have a CS degree. I love being able to enable others to reach their goals.

Science and Innovation—I may no longer be a biologist, but science still awes and fascinates me. I particularly am interested in human metabolism and our symbiosis with our microbiota.

Stories—I love stories, regardless of medium. Books, games, and movies are both food and salve for my mind. Recently I've taken a stab at writing my own stories!

Activism—We have to look out for each other. I have a lot of privilege and I try to use my circumstances to help others. I am an advocate for women in the tech industries and LGBT rights.

Nature—I'm at my happiest when I am outside. Trees and trails are somewhat rare in NYC, but I think getting out to hike or run is necessary to my mental and emotional wellbeing.
